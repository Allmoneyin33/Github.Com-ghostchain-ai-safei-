# Privacy Policy
**ALLMONEYIN33 LLC: Ghostchain SafeFi**

Last Updated: May 3, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
Ghostchain SafeFi collects minimal personal data:
- **Financial Metadata**: Transaction hashes and settlement logs.
- **System Metrics**: Latency reports and agent performance data.
- **Authentication Data**: Provided by third-party providers (e.g., Firebase Auth).

## 2. How We Use Information
Data is used strictly for:
- Executing autonomous settlements.
- Monitoring system health via the Lab-Genie error recovery loop.
- Improving the Omni-Master v140 state engine logic.

## 3. Data Protection
Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not store hardcoded secrets or raw private keys in our state engine logs.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use Stripe for payment processing. Your financial data is handled directly by Stripe in compliance with PCI-DSS regulations.

## 5. Cookies
We use minimal session cookies to maintain your system state and authentication session.

## 6. Access to Data
You may request a summary of your vaulted transaction history at any time through the Ghostchain Master Control interface.
